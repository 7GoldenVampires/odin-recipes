Magic Wands

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups white baking chips

  • 1 package (10 ounces) pretzel rods

  • Colored candy stars or sprinkles

  • Colored sugar or edible glitter


Steps

  1. In a microwave, melt chips; stir until smooth. Dip each pretzel rod halfway into melted chips; allow excess to drip off. Sprinkle coatings with candy stars and colored sugar. Place pretzels on waxed paper; let stand until dry. Store in an airtight container.
